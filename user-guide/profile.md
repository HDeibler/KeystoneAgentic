# Your Profile

Your profile contains your personal information, preferences, and account settings. This guide shows you how to view and update your profile.

---

## Accessing Your Profile

### From Anywhere in the App
1. Click your **name** in the top-right corner
2. Select **Profile** from the dropdown menu
3. Your profile page opens

### What You'll See
- Your name and email
- Your current role in the organization
- Organization membership information
- Account security settings

---

## Profile Information

### Display Name
**What it is**: Your full name as it appears throughout the app

**How to change it**:
1. Click **Edit Profile**
2. Update the **Name** field
3. Click **Save Changes**

**Where it appears**:
- Navigation bar (top-right)
- Team members list
- Conversation history (as message author)
- Activity logs

**Note**: Changing your name updates it everywhere instantly. Other users will see your new name.

---

### Email Address
**What it is**: Your login email and contact address

**How to change it**:
1. Click **Edit Profile**
2. Update the **Email** field
3. Click **Save Changes**
4. Check your **new email** for a verification link
5. Click the link to confirm

**Important**:
- Your email is your username for login
- After changing, use the **new email** to log in
- If you don't verify, the change will not complete
- Only one account per email address is allowed

**Where it's used**:
- Login authentication
- Password reset emails
- Notifications and alerts
- Invoice delivery (for Admins)

---

### Role & Permissions
**What it is**: Your access level within the organization

**Role Types**:
- **Admin**: Full access to all features, settings, and billing
- **Member**: Can use AI features, manage models and knowledge base
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to organization data

**How to view your role**:
Your role badge appears on your profile page and in the team members list.

**Can you change your own role?**
No. Only **Admins** can change user roles.

**What if you need more permissions?**
Contact an Admin in your organization to request a role change.

[Learn more about roles and permissions](/docs/organization/roles-permissions)

## Account Security

### Password Management

**Changing Your Password**:
1. Go to your profile
2. Click **Change Password**
3. You'll receive a password reset email
4. Click the link in the email
5. Enter your new password (minimum 8 characters)
6. Click **Reset Password**
7. Log in with your new password

**Why we use email-based reset**:
- Extra security layer
- Verifies you control the email account
- Prevents unauthorized password changes
- Industry-standard practice

**Password Requirements**:
- Minimum 8 characters
- Recommended: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Cannot be the same as your email
- Should be unique (not used on other sites)

**Password Best Practices**:
- Use a password manager
- Enable two-factor authentication (coming soon)
- Never share your password
- Change it if you suspect compromise

## Organization Information

Your profile shows which organizations you belong to:

### Current Organization
- **Organization Name**: The org you're currently viewing
- **Your Role**: Your permission level in this org
- **Member Since**: When you joined this organization

### Switching Organizations
If you're a member of multiple organizations:
1. Click the **organization name** in the top navigation
2. Select a different organization from the dropdown
3. You'll switch to that organization's dashboard

**Note**: Each organization has separate:
- Team members
- Models and configurations
- Knowledge bases
- Billing and quotas
- Settings

## Account Status

### Active Account
- **Status**: Active
- **Meaning**: You can log in and use the platform normally
- **Action**: None needed

### Suspended Account
- **Status**: Suspended
- **Meaning**: Your access has been temporarily restricted
- **Reason**: Usually due to organization quota exceeded or billing issues
- **Action**: Contact your organization Admin or support

### Deactivated Account
- **Status**: Deactivated
- **Meaning**: Your account has been removed from the organization
- **Reason**: Admin removed you from the team
- **Action**: Contact the organization Admin to be re-invited

## Privacy & Data

### What Data We Store
- Your name and email address
- Your role and organization memberships
- Your activity logs (conversations, uploads, etc.)
- Your preferences and settings

### What We Don't Store
- Your conversations are NOT used to train AI models
- Your documents remain private to your organization
- We never share your data with third parties
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plaintext)

### Data Access
- **You**: View and edit your own profile anytime
- **Admins**: Can view your name, email, role, and activity
- **Other Members**: Can see your name and role in the team list
- **Other Organizations**: Cannot see your information

### Data Deletion
- **Deactivated Users**: Data retained for 90 days, then soft-deleted
- **Account Deletion Request**: Contact support@keystoneagentic.com
- **Organization Deletion**: All member data is archived, then deleted after 90 days

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Update Profile
**Issue**: Save button is disabled or changes don't save

**Solutions**:
- Ensure you've filled all required fields (name, email)
- Check that email format is valid (contains @ and domain)
- Clear your browser cache and try again
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if issue persists

---

### Verification Email Not Received
**Issue**: Didn't get the email verification link after changing email

**Solutions**:
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait 5-10 minutes (delivery can be delayed)
- Add noreply@keystoneagentic.com to your contacts
- Request resend from profile page
- Contact support with your old and new email addresses

---

### Forgot Password
**Issue**: Can't remember your password

**Solutions**:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for reset link
5. Click the link and set a new password
6. Log in with your new password

**Still can't log in?**
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Check spam folder for reset email
- Try a different browser
- Contact support@keystoneagentic.com

---

### Wrong Organization Showing
**Issue**: You're logged in but seeing the wrong organization

**Solutions**:
- Click the organization name in the top navigation
- Select the correct organization from the dropdown
- If the organization isn't listed, you may not be a member
- Contact that organization's Admin to be invited
