---

## Sign Up Process

### 1. Visit the Sign Up Page
Navigate to the KeystoneAgentic homepage and click **Sign Up** or go directly to the registration page.

### 2. Enter Your Organization Details

You'll need to provide:

**Organization Information**
- **Organization Name**: Your company or team name (e.g., "Acme Corporation")
- **Organization Slug**: A unique identifier for URLs (e.g., "acme-corp")
  - Must be lowercase
  - Can contain letters, numbers, and hyphens
  - Cannot be changed after creation
  - Used in public URLs and API endpoints

**Your Information**
- **Your Name**: Your full name
- **Email Address**: Your work email (used for login)
- **Password**: A strong password
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - We recommend using a password manager

### 3. Review Trial Details

All new accounts start with a **14-day trial**:
- **100,000 tokens** included
- **Up to 5 users**
- **All features** unlocked
- **No credit card** required

### 4. Complete Registration

Click **Create Organization** to finish. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard.

## Understanding Your Trial

### What's Included
Your 14-day trial gives you full access to:
- All AI models in our catalog
- Document upload and knowledge base features
- Team collaboration (up to 5 users)
- Analytics and usage tracking
- All organization settings

### Token Usage
**100,000 tokens** is approximately:
- ~75,000 words of text
- ~50-100 conversations (depending on length)
- Enough to thoroughly test the platform

### Trial Expiration
When your trial ends:
- You'll receive email reminders at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day remaining
- On expiration, you'll need to add a payment method to continue

- You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime

## Account Security

### Password Requirements
We require strong passwords:
- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of uppercase and lowercase recommended
- Include numbers and special characters for extra security

### Password Reset
If you forget your password:
1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password
5. Log in with your new credentials

### Session Management
- Sessions last 24 hours by default
- You'll be automatically logged out after 24 hours of inactivity
- You can have multiple active sessions on different devices
- Log out from **Profile** ‚Üí **Logout**

## Troubleshooting

### "Organization Slug Already Taken"
Organization slugs must be unique across the platform. Try:
- Adding your company location (e.g., "acme-nyc")
- Including your industry (e.g., "acme-consulting")
- Adding a number (e.g., "acme-corp-2")

### "Email Already Registered"
This email is already associated with an account. You can:
- **Log in** if you already have an account
- Use the **Forgot Password** flow to reset your password
- **Contact support** if you don't recognize the account

### Didn't Receive Verification Email
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@keystoneagentic.com to your contacts
- Wait 5 minutes (delivery can be delayed)
- Click **Resend Verification Email** from your profile

### Account Locked
Too many failed login attempts will temporarily lock your account for security. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or use the **Forgot Password** flow.

## Multiple Organizations

### Can I create multiple organizations?
Yes! Each email can be associated with multiple organizations. You can:
- Create a new organization from your profile
- Switch between organizations from the organization selector
- Be a member of organizations you didn't create

### Organization Switching
To switch between organizations:
1. Click the organization name in the top navigation
2. Select the organization you want to switch to
3. You'll be redirected to that organization's dashboard

**Note**: Each organization has separate:
- Billing and subscriptions
- Team members
- AI models and configurations
- Knowledge bases
- Usage quotas
- Settings
